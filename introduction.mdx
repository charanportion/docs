## What can you build with DotPortion?

Use DotPortion to power almost anything that needs a backend:

- **Public & private APIs** for your web and mobile apps
- **Internal tools & dashboards** for operations teams
- **CRON jobs & scheduled tasks** (daily, hourly, custom schedules)
- **Event-driven workflows** (webhooks, DB events, message queues)
- **AI automation**: chatbots, assistants, retrieval flows, agentic pipelines
- **Payment & billing flows**: Stripe, Razorpay, etc.
- **Notification systems**: email, SMS, Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram

If it talks HTTP or has an SDK, you can plug it into DotPortion.

<FAQBox title="Is DotPortion no-code or low-code?">
  DotPortion is **both**. You can build entire workflows using only visual nodes,
  but every node can also be opened and edited in JavaScript/TypeScript when you need
  more control. That means you never get ‚Äústuck‚Äù behind a no-code limitation.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="What kind of projects is DotPortion good for?">
  Anything that needs a backend: SaaS applications, internal tools, AI automations,
  background tasks, integrations between services, and more. If you‚Äôre wiring APIs,
  databases, and AI together, DotPortion is a strong fit.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="Can I use DotPortion with my existing frontend stack?">
  Yes. DotPortion is **frontend-agnostic**. Use it with Next.js, React, Vue, Flutter,
  mobile apps, or no-code builders like Webflow, Framer, FlutterFlow, Bubble, and others.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="Do I need to manage servers?">
  No. DotPortion runs on a **serverless architecture**. You focus on workflows and logic;
  we handle deployment, scaling, and infrastructure.
</FAQBox>
